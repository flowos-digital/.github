[Flow.OS](https://flowos.digital) helps premium brands become the answer AI engines
recommend — and measures the revenue those engines actually send. ChatGPT, Perplexity,
Gemini, Copilot and Claude now shortlist vendors *before* a buyer ever visits a website;
we make sure premium brands are on that shortlist, and that their analytics can see it.

These repositories are the **open, practitioner side** of that work — the configs,
templates and checklists we think should be free. The plumbing lives here; the strategy
lives in the engagements.

### Open tools

| Repo | What it does |
|------|--------------|
| [**ga4-ai-traffic-attribution**](https://github.com/flowos-digital/ga4-ai-traffic-attribution) | Give referrer-bearing AI-assistant traffic (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini) its own AI Referral channel in GA4 |

### The number we work on

**70.6%** of AI-referred traffic lands in GA4 as *Direct* — and those sessions convert at
**10.21%, roughly 4.1× the Direct baseline.** Most brands are making budget decisions
against a bucket that hides their highest-intent visitors. Start by giving the *measurable*
slice its own channel — see [`ga4-ai-traffic-attribution`](https://github.com/flowos-digital/ga4-ai-traffic-attribution)
and the study below.
